Food allergen awareness: A refresher


If you have a food allergy, follow these steps to increase your chances of avoiding an allergic reaction:


  • Practice prevention. Always know what you're eating and drinking.

  • Know about hidden food allergens. Some food allergens may be well hidden when used as ingredients in certain dishes.

  • Identify your allergy. Wear a medical alert bracelet that describes your allergy and carry an alert card in your wallet or purse.
